Unmanned aerial vehicle

A drone without a camera is like a body without a soul.

Unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs), sometimes known as drones, are aircraft that do not have a human pilot, crew, or passengers on board. Unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) are part of an unmanned aircraft system (UAS), which includes a ground-based controller and a communications system with the UAV. UAVs can fly under remote control by a human operator, known as remotely piloted aircraft (RPA), or with varying degrees of autonomy, such as autopilot assistance, all the way up to fully autonomous aircraft with no human interaction.
